## Encryption in OpenVine

### Nostr Protocol (Primary)
- **secp256k1** elliptic curve for keys, signatures, key exchange
- **NIP-44**: ChaCha20-Poly1305 for encrypted DMs
- **NIP-04**: AES-256-CBC (legacy, deprecated)
- **NIP-59**: Gift wrapping with ephemeral keys

### ProofMode
- **RSA-2048** + **SHA-256** for video attestation
- **PGP/OpenPGP** signatures (dart_pg, openpgp packages)

### Storage
- **iOS Keychain** for private key storage (OS-provided, exempt)
